The Meaning of Baptism

Baptism is an outward expression of an inward faith. It symbolizes the death, burial, and resurrection of Jesus Christ and our identification with Him. When we are immersed in the water, it represents being buried with Christ. When we are raised from the water, it symbolizes our resurrection to new life in Him.

What Baptism Represents:

  • Death to Sin: Our old life of sin is dead and buried with Christ
  • Cleansing: We are washed clean from our sins through faith in Jesus
  • New Life: We rise to walk in newness of life, empowered by the Holy Spirit
  • Public Declaration: We publicly identify ourselves as followers of Jesus Christ
